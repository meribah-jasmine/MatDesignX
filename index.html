Our Core Technology

Density Functional Theory (DFT)

A powerful quantum mechanical method to predict the electronic structure and properties of materials from first principles.

  • Predicting Material Behavior: Calculate properties before synthesis.
  • Optimizing Processes: Understand synthesis and processing parameters.
  • Designing Novel Materials: Engineer materials with targeted properties.

Non-Equilibrium Green's Function (NEGF)

An indispensable formalism for simulating quantum transport and carrier dynamics in nanoscale electronic devices.

  • Device Performance: Model I-V characteristics of ultra-small transistors.
  • Optoelectronics: Simulate efficiency in LEDs and solar cells.
  • Emerging Tech: Analyze transport in spintronics and 2D materials.
